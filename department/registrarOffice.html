‚è∞ 2. Delayed Registration ng Birth Certificate

Pag-register ng Birth Certificate after 30 days from birth
Basic Requirements (Below 18 years old)
  • Certificate of Live Birth (4 copies, accomplished at signed)
  • Affidavit of Delayed Registration
  • Negative Certification from PSA
  • 2 Documentary Evidence (Baptismal Certificate, School Record, etc.)
  • Affidavit of Two Disinterested Persons (with ID)
  • Marriage Certificate ng parents (kung married)
  • National ID/ePhilID/Digital ID
  • Barangay Certification as proof of residency
  • 2x2 unedited front facing photo
  • Personal appearance ng parents
  • 10 days posting
  • Registrant's Affidavit
Additional Requirements (18 years old and above)
  • Lahat ng requirements sa taas PLUS:
  • Personal appearance ng document owner (registrant)
  • Affidavit of Admission of Paternity (para sa non-marital)
  • Affidavit to Use the Surname of the Father (para sa non-marital)
Additional Requirements for Special Cases
  • 18+ years old: Personal appearance ng document owner
  • Foreign parent: Valid Passport, BI Clearance, o ACR-I Card + Birth Certificate ng parents
  • Deceased document owner: Death Certificate + Notarized Affidavit stating deceased na ang registrant
