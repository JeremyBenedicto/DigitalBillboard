üì¢ 4. Feedback at Complaints Mechanism

Paano Magbigay ng Feedback
  • Answer the client feedback form and drop it to the designated drop box at the Public Assistance and Complaint Desk
  • O mag-email sa: gabnecija_50@yahoo.com/gabnecijahrmo@gmail.com
Processing ng Feedback
  • Every Friday the assigned Officers of the Day open the drop box and compiles and recorded all feedback submitted
  • Feedback requiring answers are forwarded to the relevant offices
  • They are required to answer within three (3) days of the receipt of the feedback
  • The answer of the office is then relayed to the citizen
Paano Mag-file ng Complaint
  • Answer the client Complaint form and drop it at the designated drop box in front of the Public Assistance and Complaint Desk
  • Complaints can also be filed via text, call and email. Make sure to provide the following information:
    • Name of person being complained
    • Incident
    • Evidence
Processing ng Complaints
  • The Municipal Administrator opens the complaints drop box on a daily basis and evaluate each complaint
  • Upon evaluation the Municipal Administrator shall start the investigation and forward the complaint to the relevant office for their explanation
  • The Municipal Administrator will create a report after the investigation and shall submit to the Local Chief Executive for appropriate action
  • The Municipal Administrator will give the feedback to the client
