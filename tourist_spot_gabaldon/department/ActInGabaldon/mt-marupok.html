Mt. Marupok stands as one of Gabaldon's most treasured natural landmarks, offering adventurers an authentic mountain experience away from the crowds. This hidden gem provides a perfect escape into nature's embrace, where pristine trails wind through lush forests and lead to breathtaking panoramic views.

The mountain's raw, undeveloped trails offer hikers a chance to experience the true wilderness of Nueva Ecija. Every step reveals new wonders - from diverse flora and fauna to stunning rock formations that have been shaped by centuries of natural forces.

Whether you're a seasoned hiker seeking your next challenge or a nature lover looking for tranquility, Mt. Marupok delivers an unforgettable experience that connects you deeply with the natural world.
